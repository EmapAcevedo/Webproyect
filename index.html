WORKIN' ON IT

"Helping organize your daily life"

Workin' on it boards, lists and cards enable you to organize and prioritize your daily activities, having them in a better look and accesible way in order you to stay in touch with your activities. Workin' on it will replace your ordinary agenda to a new interface were you can have all your activities in one place, but keeping it simple like your old fashion agenda and making it user friendly.

NOT JUST NOTES

Working on it isn't just notes, you can aslo put your activities and task to do for the day, at the end you can check if you did it or not, also it will notify you if your task is about to end and you haven't do it.

We try to make people to be more productive, some people have trouble getting their things done and we thing that workin' on it could make people organize them better so they could get their activities faster and easier. Some people say they like to use a phisical agenda and working on it tries to make the experience like your old fashion agenda.

READY FOR YOU

Easy access to your notes when you need it

You could access all your notes all in one place, Workin' on it will notice you when you have to do your choirs and assigments. On time notifications you could manage, delete, edit and create. Activities will be easier
